Taylor Swift Bio

Taylor Alison Swift (born December 13, 1989) is an American singer-songwriter. Her narrative songwriting, which is often inspired by her personal experiences, has received widespread media coverage and critical praise. Aspiring to become a country singer, Swift moved to Nashville in 2004 and signed with Sony/ATV Music Publishing as a songwriter; she further signed a recording deal with Big Machine in 2005.
